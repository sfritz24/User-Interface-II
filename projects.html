My Projects

This is an original drawing I did for fun. I drew it from scratch and proceeded to draw it digitally. The idea I had behind it was that Ditto is essentially a copycat and so I decided to put a cat costume on him and just call him "Dittocat" instead of copycat. This was also my second attempt at digital art and I'm very proud of it.

I took a pottery class in college and this was my largest project that I did. It is a full dinnerwear set so there are cups, bowls, and plates. I only needed to make 4 of each but they had to be roughly the same size so I ended up making 6 total. I did that so I could pick out the best ones for submitting my finial project. There are two other pieces in the background that you can see that I did roughly at the same time. Those were also large in terms of size but they were individual projects.

This is a custom bedframe that I desined myself. I put in many, many hours trying to figure everthing out in terms of measurements. I knew that I wanted some desks built into it and I knew that I wanted a dog bed on one side. I found the idea online and made it my own. The original used premade structures and the bed was sitting above the top of the platform. There were other things different but I just took that idea and transformed it to fit my needs and wants.

This is as mask I made for Halloween one year. This mask is custom made for my face. I also made the body paint. I did that beacuse it was easier and cheaper for me to make my own waterproof makeup. I will most certianly be making more various masks in the future. This mask was spcifically meant for a tiger costume. Everyone that saw me that night loved it and anyone who could get close enough really enjoyed the fact that the nose had a very realistic texture, even if it might have freaked a few people out.

I made this bracelet and many others like it to sell one day. There isn't a real story behing why I started making bracelets. The simplest explanation is that I like to try and learn new things. These kinds of bracelets are relatively easy and inexpensive to make. It was an easy way for me to learn something new without paying an arm and leg. One thing that makes this bracelet stand out is that it glows under UV light.

This is a very old desk that we had for a long time. It was rather ugly but it got the job done so we weren't going to complain. It helps that people didn't see it regularly. For a long time it was in storage, but when we wanted to move our desktop computer, we decided we should put the computer on an actual desk. Since the desk was going to be out in a common space, we wanted to give it a face lift. We were originally going to just paint it but after we sanded it and saw the wood, we ended up staining it. It's not sell-worthy but we are happy with the end result.
